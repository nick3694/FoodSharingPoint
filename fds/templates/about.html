About us...

Food sharing point’s mission is to reduce food waste and to help needy people.

Our Vision

Food Sharing Point is a no-profit organization whose goals are to help needy people in easily find food and to encourage people to donate almost expired food. We collaborate with supermarkets in which food is collected thanks to volunteers’ work: it is possible to book and to withdraw food whenever you want. The advantage for donators is that in return for donated food the can have a special discount our supermarkets.
